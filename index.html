Our Process

Communication is everything. We know that hiring someone to do develop a project can be intimidating. It is sort of like hiring someone to translate your book to a foreign language that you personally don't speak and therefore have no way of verifying that the work is done right. At Meddo we have understanding for this and therefore provide each client with a individual project portal where the project is thorough defined and our designers and developers update the progress continuously from beginning to end. And we will not overwhelm you with tech jargon. As a client you will be able to leave comments in our shared portal, making it easy to follow up and keep all communication in one place.
